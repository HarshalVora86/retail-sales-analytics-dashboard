## рҹ“Ң Project Overview

**RetailSense** is a comprehensive Excel-based analytics project built on a dataset of **250 retail transactions** across **50 unique customers**, **5 regions**, and **3 product categories**.

The goal was to extract meaningful business intelligence using advanced Excel features вҖ” from formula-based analysis to interactive dashboards and statistical regression.

## рҹ”¬ Analysis Sheet

> **8 clearly labeled sections covering all required Excel functions**

- **Date & Time Functions** вҖ” TODAY, NOW, DATEDIF, EOMONTH used to compute today's date, current time, days since first transaction (762), and last day of current month.

  ![Date Time](screenshots/date-time-functions.png)

- **Key Summary Metrics** вҖ” Total Revenue вӮ№2,29,192.47 В· 251 Transactions В· AOV вӮ№916.77 В· 50 Unique Customers В· Most sold product: Bookshelf.

  ![Key Metrics](screenshots/key-summary-metrics.png)

  ![High Value](screenshots/high-value-customers.png)

- **TEXT Functions (Abbreviations)** вҖ” LEFT, MID, FIND, UPPER used to generate customer initials (e.g. P.B.) and 3-letter product short codes (e.g. MON, LAP, BOO).

## рҹ“ү Linear Regression

> **Unit Price вҶ’ Total Revenue | RВІ = 0.698**

![Regression](screenshots/linear-regression-output.png)

- **Regression Equation:** `Total Amount = -14.34 + (3.14 Г— Unit Price)`

| Metric | Value | Interpretation |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Multiple R | 0.835 | Strong positive correlation |
| R Square | 0.698 | Unit Price explains 69.8% of revenue variation |
| Significance F | 2.03E-66 | Model is highly statistically significant |
| Coefficient | 3.14 | Every вӮ№1 increase in price вҶ’ вӮ№3.14 more revenue |
